FAQ:

1. How can I order a drink?

A: On the main page, simply drag and drop the drink of your choice to the Order List and press Pay.

2. How can I add a favourite?

A: On the main page, simply drag and drop the picture of a beer glass to the drink of your choice and it will be added to your favourites.

3. How can I undo my order?

A: On the main page, simply press "undo" and undo the drink of your choice.

4. How can I cancel my order?

A: On the main page, simply press "cancel" and cancel your order.

5. How can I browse my recent purchases?

A: On the main page, simply press recent purchases.

6. How can I see the current stock of a drink?

A: On the main page, simply drag and drop the drink of your choice to the Order List and press Pay.

7. How can I find out more about a specific drink?

A: On the main page, press the "i" information sign on your designated product, to find out more of the specific product.

8. How can I change the language?

A: On the main page, press the language symbols to toggle between languages.
